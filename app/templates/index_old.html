Our Mission Statement

This pandemic has shown us all too clearly that access to medical resources is a luxury. We will create a
community of doctors and patients where everyone can access healthcare in a timely manner.

Learn more »

Crowdsourced Healthcare

By using forums to “crowdsource” medical knowledge, we can provide the user with an approximation of their illness and removing the need for a lengthy appointment. In addition, crowdsourcing healthcare will give disadvantaged communities greater and more affordable access to medical information.

View details »

Large Medical
Database

One of the pressing concerns in the field of medicine is a small sample size on the symptoms of certain illnesses. With our database, and in compliance with HIPPA laws, we can provide medical experts resources and references for diseases.

View details »

Safety &
Encryption

Patient privacy is very important to us and we understand our obligation to keep patient data secure. Our encryption will ensure that sensitive data can ONLY be access by the end user and practicing physicians.
