In‧no‧và‧re

to innovate, to renew

Here at Innovo, it is our fundamental belief that healthcare is a universal right that must be acessible to everyone. In pursuit of this goal, we have built a comprehensive platform for the information age, bringing doctors and patients together into a single community. Together, we will innovate our way towards greater healthcare accessbility.

Crowdsourced Healthcare

Our platform connects people directly to doctors and each other, building a community dedicated to wellbeing.

Advanced Security


Security is our upmost priority. All passwords are salted and hashed, and all sensitive information is encrypted.

We maintain large open-source sets of de-idenitified medical data, pushing the frontiers of scientific research.
